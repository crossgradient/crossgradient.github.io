Sea Level is the New Oxygen

Vision

We are all taught that climbing higher mountain tops is a great metaphor of business achievement. Truth is, that metaphor is somewhat broken : that coveted peak of your dreams only offers you scarce oxygen to breathe, no surface to run and increased ultraviolet radiation, affecting your vision.
That destination is simply wrong.

We believe most businesses and governments should adopt the opposite approach : escape the top and increase field impact !
At every point of your critical path to global efficiency, we simply use good math and let data gradients shape your trajectory. We help you find the steepest slope to reach sea level at full speed, in the right state, at the right destination.

A few things we can do for you

  • Help you figure out if you have a data problem
  • Help you figure out if you have a data problem
  • Audit and improve your data infrastructure
  • Design healthy data governance processes
  • Automate the categorization of massive text, image and video content
  • Build your recommender system
  • Automate your predictive marketing efforts
  • Bootstrap your business intelligence practice
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Optimize your ad inventory usage
  • Understand your customers' sentiment towards your brand
  • Help you innovate

Team

We are a distributed team of seasoned data scientists, strategists and software makers that can quickly organize around your core needs.

Some would call us math freaks, others would call us data nerds, the truth is you can call us any way you want ! Because building great data products always requires experimentation, you should not expect us to pull white doves out of your sleeves. Instead, we take your problems and relentlessly iterate on them until they turn into competitive advantages.

We can help you reason about your data for an hour over the phone or add predictive capabilities to your product over an extended partnership.
So reach out and tell us your story !
